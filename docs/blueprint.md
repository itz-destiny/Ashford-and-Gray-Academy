# **App Name**: Ashford and Gray Fusion Academy

## Core Features:

- User Authentication and Roles: Secure login and role-based access for admins, organizers, instructors, students, and guests.
- Course and Event Scheduling: Calendar integration, automated reminders, and timetable management.
- Live Classes and Webinars: Integration with Zoom or Google Meet for live video sessions. Students use AI tools to summarize or review the meetings afterward.
- Automated Assessment and Certification: Automatic quiz generation, auto-grading and downloadable certifications after completion of the course
- Payment Gateway Integration: Secure payment processing using multiple gateways (Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe).
- Resource Library: Centralized repository for PDFs, slides, videos, and images.
- Messaging and Notifications: In-app chat, group discussions, push/email alerts for announcements and updates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#34495E) to convey trust and knowledge.
- Background color: Light gray (#F4F6F6), providing a clean and professional canvas.
- Accent color: Vibrant orange (#E67E22) for key actions and highlights, symbolizing energy and engagement.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for a modern and neutral feel suitable for body text and headlines
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif with a computerized feel, ideal for headlines, and suitable for shorter body texts too
- Clean, professional icons that represent learning, events, and community, designed for clarity and ease of understanding.
- Modern, intuitive layout with a focus on clear navigation and accessibility.
- Subtle transitions and feedback animations to enhance user experience and guide interactions.
